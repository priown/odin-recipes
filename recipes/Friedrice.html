Jollof Rice Recipes

Description

Fried rice is a flavorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, oil, and seasonings. It is known for its rich taste, colorful ingredients, and slightly smoky aroma that comes from cooking the rice in a hot pan.

This dish is popular around the world and is often served with chicken, shrimp, or eggs. It is easy to prepare and is a great way to turn simple ingredients into a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked chicken pieces (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and cook until they become soft.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs.
  5. Add the cooked rice into the pan and mix everything together.
  6. Add soy sauce, curry powder, and salt to taste.
  7. Stir everything well and cook for a few more minutes.
  8. Serve hot with chicken or any preferred protein.
