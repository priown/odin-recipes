Jollof Rice Recipes

Description

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce. It is known for its vibrant red color, delicious aroma, and flavorful blend of spices. The dish is commonly prepared with ingredients like tomatoes, onions, pepper, and seasoning, and it is often served with chicken, fish, or fried plantains.

This dish is a favorite at parties, celebrations, and family gatherings across countries like Nigeria and Ghana. Jollof rice is loved for its smoky taste and comforting flavor, making it one of the most famous African dishes enjoyed both locally and internationally.

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Cups chicken stock
  • Maggi
  • Thyme
  • Curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes, and red bell pepper,until smooth.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pot and sauté the chopped onions for a few minutes.
  3. Add the tomato paste and fry for 2–3 minutes to deepen the flavor.
  4. Pour in the blended tomato mixture and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add thyme, curry powder, salt, Maggi and chicken stock. Stir well.
  6. Add the washed rice into the sauce and mix properly.
  7. Cover the pot and cook on low heat until the rice is soft and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Stir the rice gently and serve hot with chicken, fish, or fried plantains.
